How publishing works

Your works are saved in this browser. To make them appear on your live site, you export a file and upload it to GitHub. Netlify then updates your site automatically within ~30 seconds.

Step 1. Click Export fics.json below. A file downloads to your computer.
Step 2. Go to your GitHub repo → open the fics folder → click index.json → click the pencil ✏️ icon to edit.
Step 3. Select all the text in the editor and delete it. Then open your downloaded index.json in Notepad, copy everything, and paste it in.
Step 4. Click Commit changes. Wait 30 seconds. Your site is updated!
